[cite_start]It augments a Strong Mask R-CNN network to incorporate human-specified partial boundaries (human attention maps/sketches)[cite: 52, 53, 54]. [cite_start]This human-assisted approach can generate "higher-quality segmentation masks" [cite: 51] [cite_start]and significantly outperforms state-of-the-art methods in AP-Mask metrics[cite: 55]. [cite_start]While applied to nuclei segmentation, its core contribution of generating "effective prompts to guide segmentation" [cite: 34] [cite_start]and "automated adaptability for diverse segmentation tasks" [cite: 36] is highly relevant. [cite_start]While it notes challenges for deep networks with certain regularization techniques [cite: 45][cite_start], its premise is that removing the background can "boost data quality and increase model performance"[cite: 39].
